PASS 3 — Answer & Snippet Optimization

Optimize for Google featured snippet extraction.

Rules:
- Generate a 40-word featured snippet for each section
- Explicit Yes/No for Boolean questions:
  * "Is root canal painful?" → "No. Modern root canal treatment uses local anesthesia..."
  * "Can children get dental implants?" → "No. Dental implants require..."
- Use numeric values or truth ranges:
  * "Dental implant cost in Vellore: ₹25,000–₹45,000 per tooth"
  * "Root canal treatment duration: 45–90 minutes per sitting"
  * "Braces treatment timeline: 12–24 months for most patients"
- Bold answers only (not questions or context)
- Plural nouns must include examples:
  * "dental treatments" → "dental treatments (implants, root canals, crowns)"
  * "gum diseases" → "gum diseases (gingivitis, periodontitis)"
- Lists must share part of speech:
  * ✓ "Benefits include: faster healing, lower cost, minimal pain"
  * ✗ "Benefits include: healing is faster, costs less, no pain"
- Place "if" conditions after declarations:
  * ✓ "Dental implants last 20+ years if properly maintained"
  * ✗ "If properly maintained, dental implants last 20+ years"

Snippet Formats:
- Definition: "[Entity] is [definition]. [Key attribute 1]. [Key attribute 2]."
- Process: "[Process] involves [step count] steps: [step 1], [step 2], [step 3]."
- Comparison: "[Option A] costs [X] and takes [Y]. [Option B] costs [A] and takes [B]."

Rewrite content accordingly. Preserve all JSX syntax.
